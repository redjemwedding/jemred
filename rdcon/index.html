How It Works

From Clock-In to Cloud Insight

Three steps from biometric scan to real-time management dashboard — fully automated, zero manual entry.

01

CAPTURE — Biometric Scan

Employees check in using fingerprint, face recognition, or card tap on the RDConnect biometric device. Fast, accurate, and tamper-proof — no buddy punching, no manual registers.

02

CONNECT — Instant Cloud Sync

Every check-in syncs to the cloud in real-time. Your HR team, managers, and admins see attendance data live — from any device, anywhere. No waiting for end-of-day reports.

03

MANAGE — Powerful Insights

Generate attendance reports, manage leaves, set alerts, and track device health — all from one clean dashboard. Role-based access keeps every stakeholder informed.

Core Capabilities

Everything HR & Operations Actually Needs

Built for businesses in the UAE and beyond — accurate, secure, and scalable from 10 to 10,000 employees.

Fast & Accurate Biometric Capture

Fingerprint and face recognition with sub-second response. No buddy punching, no proxy attendance — just accurate, tamper-proof data every time.

Seamless Cloud Sync

Every check-in syncs to the cloud instantly. Access live attendance data from anywhere — web, mobile, or desktop — with zero configuration required.

Powerful Insights & Reports

Generate daily, weekly, and monthly attendance reports in one click. Export to Excel, PDF, or integrate directly with your payroll system.

Multi-Device Support

Manage up to hundreds of biometric devices across multiple locations from a single dashboard. Monitor device health, connectivity, and firmware updates centrally.

Role-Based Access Control

HR managers, department heads, and admins each see only what they need. Granular permissions ensure the right people have the right level of access.

Smart Alerts & Notifications

Set automated alerts for late arrivals, early departures, and device outages. Keep managers informed in real-time without manual monitoring.
