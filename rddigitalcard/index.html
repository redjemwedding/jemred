Core Capabilities

Everything Your Sales Team Actually Needs

Built for relationship-driven sales in the UAE and beyond — where timing, compliance, and trust are everything.

Privacy-First Lead Capture

Consent shown before any data collection. GDPR and UAE PDPL compliant by design — your leads are warm, never alarmed.

Automated Reminder Engine

Watches lead behavior — views, clicks, form fills — and auto-queues personalized follow-up reminders. Never miss a warm lead.

Salesforce & CRM Sync

Leads flow directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, or your CRM. Records created, tagged, and assigned automatically — zero manual entry.
