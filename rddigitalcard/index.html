How It Works

From First Tap to Closed Deal

Every interaction on your digital card is tracked, captured, and routed to the right workflow — automatically.

01
Share Your Card

Share your RD Digital Card via QR code, NFC tap, or direct link. Prospects access your full profile, contact details, and company links — all in one beautifully designed card.

02
Consent-Based Capture

A privacy notice is shown to visitors. When they choose to submit their details, their data is captured with full compliance. Every click is logged — with permission. No grey areas.

03

Your dashboard automatically queues follow-up reminders based on lead activity, interest level, and timing. Your sales team connects with the right people at exactly the right moment.

Core Capabilities

Everything Your Sales Team Actually Needs

Built for relationship-driven sales in the UAE and beyond — where timing and trust are everything.

Privacy-First Lead Capture

All data collected with explicit user consent. A clear privacy notice is shown before any form submission — keeping you fully compliant and your leads warm, not alarmed.

Automated Reminder Engine

The system watches lead behavior — views, clicks, shares, form fills — and automatically queues personalized follow-up reminders for the card owner. Never miss a warm lead again.

Salesforce & CRM Sync

New leads flow directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, or your preferred CRM. Contact records are created, tagged, and assigned automatically — zero manual data entry for your team.

Engagement Analytics

See who viewed your card, which links they clicked, how long they spent, and where they came from. Turn engagement data into sales intelligence with real-time dashboards.

Multi-User Team Cards

Deploy cards across your entire sales team from one admin panel. Each rep gets their own branded card, their own lead stream, and their own follow-up queue — all centrally managed.

Relationship Intelligence

Build better relationships without being pushy. The platform tracks your entire interaction history with each lead, so you always know the right moment to reach out — and with the right message.
