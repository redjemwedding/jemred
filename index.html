Our Love Story

Where it All Began

06 Jan 2017

We first saw each other, met, and danced back in 2016 in Quezon City, Philippines. It all started with a chocolate bar from Bali — a sweet gesture that sparked something special between us, leading us to realize we were destined to be forever.

Starts of infinite love.

Creating Memories Together

First Spark of Love. When we started our journey, our life became our everyday but in 2019, she moved to Dubai, and our journey as a long-distance couple began. For four years, we held on — through calls, messages, and quiet prayers — trusting that love, patience, and faith in God would carry us through. In 2023, just before Valentine’s Day, I flew to Dubai — not just to visit, but because deep in our hearts, we knew it was time. Everything began falling into place, and we could truly feel God’s hand at work.

The Proposal: Where ‘Until Death Do Us Part’ Began

28 March 2024

Then came Paris — the city of love. As the Eiffel Tower glittered behind us and the cool night air wrapped around the moment, everything slowed down. With a trembling hand and a heart full of certainty, the question was asked... and with a smile, tears, and a love that had only grown stronger through the years, the answer was 'yes.' Our forever officially began under the Parisian sky..
